July 16, 2026

Thursday

⛈️ Weather — Tucson

⚠️ Flood Watch in effect until 5:00 AM MST Friday (July 17). NWS Tucson has issued a Flood Watch covering the Tucson Metro Area, Catalina and Rincon Mountains, Baboquivari Mountains, Tohono O'odham Nation, Upper Santa Cruz River Valley/Altar Valley, and portions of Pinal County. Scattered thunderstorms capable of producing very heavy rainfall in short periods are expected to drive flash flooding and significant flow in area washes, creeks, and rivers beginning this afternoon.

Today: Showers and thunderstorms likely. Partly sunny with a high near 97, falling to around 91 by afternoon. South-southwest wind 1–6 mph. 70% chance of precipitation. Tonight: Showers and thunderstorms likely continuing, low around 72. Tomorrow (Friday): Storms remain likely through the day, high near 90, 70% chance of precipitation. The active monsoon pattern persists into the weekend, with storm chances gradually decreasing Saturday (30%) and Sunday (20%) as conditions slowly improve.

🏛️ Government

Pima County supervisors hear jail treatment data, water spike, food program threat, and border wall discussion. New Pima County data presented at Tuesday's Board of Supervisors meeting suggests that jail-based treatment programs may be helping reduce overdose deaths, though a county official's word choice drew scrutiny. The board also heard that a food-access program faces elimination under federal funding cuts, received a briefing on an unusual spike in water use, and discussed border wall-related matters, according to AZ Luminaria.

AZ Luminaria

Amphi School District to ask voters to approve property sales. Amphitheater Public Schools is placing a measure on the November 3 ballot seeking voter authorization to sell, lease, or exchange land and buildings the district no longer needs, according to Tucson Local Media.

Tucson Local Media

Judge allows Arizona GOP legislative leaders to help defend Prop. 314 in court. Arizona's top Republican legislative leaders have been granted permission to intervene in the federal court defense of Proposition 314, the voter-approved border enforcement law, as the ACLU challenges its constitutionality. Questions also remain over Attorney General Kris Mayes' role in the defense, according to the Arizona Daily Star.

Arizona Daily Star

Operation Stonegarden funding splits Pima County law enforcement agencies. Oro Valley, Marana, and Sahuarita continue to accept federal Operation Stonegarden border security grants, while the City of Tucson and Pima County have cut ties with the program, according to Tucson Spotlight.

Tucson Spotlight

Arizona group home watchdog created after Hacienda Healthcare rape case faces defunding. An independent oversight program established to protect people with developmental disabilities in group homes will cease operations at the end of 2026 unless new funding is found, after being cut in Arizona's most recent bipartisan state budget, according to the Arizona Mirror.

Arizona Mirror

Oro Valley mayoral race takes shape ahead of primary. Vice Mayor Melanie Barrett and former Pima County Sheriff Mark Napier are facing off in the race for Oro Valley mayor, with the July 21 Arizona Primary Election approaching, according to Tucson Local Media.

Tucson Local Media

🚨 Public Safety

Monsoon flooding closes Lago del Oro Parkway stretch; eastside road also damaged. The Pima County Sheriff's Department warned drivers Wednesday night of flooding on Lago del Oro Parkway between Rail N Road and Golder Ranch Drive. Separately, a portion of an eastside Tucson road remains closed after recent monsoon rains appear to have lifted and buckled a section of asphalt, according to KGUN 9 and KVOA.

KGUN 9, KVOA

Shaw Peak Fire burns in Chiricahua Mountains with no containment. A new wildfire ignited in the northern Chiricahua Mountains within the Coronado National Forest, with firefighters working to contain the blaze. No containment had been reported as of Wednesday evening, according to KVOA and KOLD.

KVOA, KOLD

Murder trial continues for man accused of killing Pima County good Samaritan. Testimony continued Wednesday in the trial of Jack Daryl Upchurch, charged with first-degree murder and multiple other counts in connection with the death of Paul Clifford of Redington. The victim's daughter testified during Wednesday's proceedings, according to KVOA and KOLD.

KVOA, KOLD

Tucson man charged in westside bus stop attack. A Tucson man is in custody after allegedly attacking a woman at a westside bus stop, according to KVOA.

KVOA

🏗️ Development & Business

Gibson Food Hall & Market quietly opens in downtown Tucson. The new Gibson Food Hall & Market has opened in the space formerly occupied by Johnny Gibson Downtown Market, bringing four restaurants, a grocery and convenience store, a bar, a coffee shop, and a speakeasy under one roof, according to the Arizona Daily Star.

Arizona Daily Star

Santa Rita Park grand reopening pushed back by shipping delay. The City of Tucson announced that Santa Rita Park's anticipated mid-August reopening will be delayed due to a shipping delay affecting a critical piece of electrical equipment. No new opening date has been announced, according to KVOA and KOLD.

KVOA, KOLD

Major construction begins at busy Tucson interchange. Work has started on a significant infrastructure project at one of Tucson's busiest interchanges, according to KVOA. Details on the specific location and scope were not available in the published summary.

KVOA

🎉 Community & Events

World Cup draws Tucsonans together across the city. With the 2026 FIFA Men's World Cup — the first to feature 48 teams across Canada, Mexico, and the United States — underway this summer, fans from Tucson's diverse communities are gathering at bars, restaurants, and community spaces to watch matches together, according to AZ Luminaria.

AZ Luminaria

Southwest Record and Vintage Fair moves to air-conditioned La Rosa venue. The annual Southwest Record and Vintage Fair is set to take place at downtown Tucson music venue La Rosa this summer — the first year the event will be held in an air-conditioned space — according to the Tucson Weekly.
