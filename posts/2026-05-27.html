May 27, 2026

Wednesday

🚨 Public Safety

AZ DPS trooper fatally shoots woman during Memorial Day traffic stop on I-10 east of Tucson. A 39-year-old woman was shot and killed by an Arizona Department of Public Safety trooper during a traffic stop on Interstate 10 on Memorial Day (Monday), east of Tucson. Authorities have since identified the victim, though DPS has not publicly detailed the circumstances that led to the use of lethal force. The incident remains under investigation.

Arizona Daily Star

Man killed, woman injured in southeast Tucson holiday weekend crash. A 21-year-old man was killed and a 24-year-old woman injured when their vehicle struck a light pole on Tucson's southeast side early Sunday morning, police say. The crash occurred during the Memorial Day holiday weekend; no further details on the cause have been released.

Arizona Daily Star

Sierra Vista man airlifted to Tucson hospital after stabbing; suspect at large. A 49-year-old man suffered serious stab wounds Tuesday afternoon at a residence in the 4400 block of East Camino Principal near Sierra Vista and was airlifted to a Tucson hospital after initial treatment at Canyon Vista Medical Center. Cochise County Sheriff's deputies say the suspect — a 30-year-old man — fled on foot before authorities arrived. No physical description or identity of the suspect has been released.

🏛️ Government

U of A directs faculty to notify campus police if non-university law enforcement arrives on campus. University of Arizona officials with the Office of Public Safety told faculty senators this spring to immediately contact the UA Police Department if "non-university law enforcement" officers appear on campus and attempt to enter classrooms or other spaces. The policy reflects growing institutional concern across universities nationwide about the presence of federal enforcement personnel. UA officials did not specify what action UAPD would take following notification.

Arizona Daily Star

🏗️ Development & Business

Pascua Yaqui Tribe cemetery to be preserved within Marana's 750-acre mixed-use development. A Pascua Yaqui-owned burial site containing about two dozen graves, located west of Interstate 10 and north of Twin Peaks Road, will be left undisturbed as developer Bourne Partners builds a roughly 750-acre mixed-use project around it in Marana. Town officials say a 50-foot protective buffer will surround the cemetery and access will remain open throughout construction. The project will add a mix of residential and commercial uses as Marana continues its rapid growth northwest of Tucson.

KGUN 9

🎉 Community & Events

UA men's golf team heads to NCAA Championships after record-setting regional win. The University of Arizona men's golf team won the NCAA Marana Regional at The Gallery Golf Club with a 49-under par team total — one of the strongest regional performances in recent history — to earn a spot in the NCAA Division I Championships. The Wildcats will compete May 29–June 3 at the Omni La Costa Resort & Spa in Carlsbad, California. Head coach Jim Anderson's squad enters the national tournament with strong momentum after a dominant Big 12 season.
