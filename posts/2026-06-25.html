Management Training Corporation disputes DHS figures on Marana prison ICE conversion. The Town of Marana acknowledged community concerns following a Department of Homeland Security notice proposing to repurpose an existing Marana prison as an ICE detention facility. The Management Training Corporation, which operates the facility, stated the DHS notice contained incorrect capacity figures, leaving the scope of the proposal in dispute.

Tucson Local Media / Explorer News

Trump's second border wall could destroy the only habitat for three species endemic to Quitobaquito Springs. A proposed second layer of border wall in Organ Pipe Cactus National Monument — roughly 200 miles west of Tucson — would likely destroy the last wild habitat of the Sonoyta mud turtle, Sonoyta pupfish, and Quitobaquito springsnail, three species found nowhere else on Earth. Conservationists and University of Arizona researchers warn that construction would disrupt the springs' delicate hydrology, and that REAL ID Act waivers used to fast-track the project bypass the Endangered Species Act entirely.

USPS confirms it will halt mail ballot delivery in states that refuse to turn over voter rolls. Postmaster General David Steiner testified Wednesday before the Senate that the Postal Service will not deliver absentee ballots in states that decline to provide voter lists under a proposed Trump administration rule. Arizona's widespread use of mail voting makes the state directly vulnerable if the rule takes effect and the state refuses to comply — potentially affecting ballot delivery ahead of November midterm elections.

🏗️ Development & Business

Southern Arizona building a next-generation defense and technology ecosystem. A new analysis finds that Southern Arizona's defense economy is evolving beyond its traditional anchor institutions — Raytheon, Davis-Monthan Air Force Base, and Fort Huachuca — toward a broader ecosystem integrating AI, hypersonics, and space capabilities. The region is positioning itself to attract new federal contracts and private investment as national security priorities shift toward advanced technology.
